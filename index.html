About Us

Building Bridges in Global Trade Since 2009

Liuyang Chengjin Trading Co., Ltd. was established with a vision to create seamless connections between manufacturers and markets worldwide. Based in Liuyang, Hunan Province, China, we have grown into a comprehensive trading company serving customers across Europe, America, and Asia.

Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of over 500 international partners. We specialize in a diverse range of products including daily necessities, handicrafts, hardware and building materials, agricultural by-products, and electronic products.

In addition to our trading business, we have expanded into mobile application development, delivering innovative management solutions through Google Play and the Apple App Store. Our dedicated team of developers creates applications that help businesses streamline operations and engage with their customers effectively.
