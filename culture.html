Our Foundation

Vision & Mission

Guided by clear purpose and unwavering values, we strive to make a positive impact on global trade.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted bridge connecting Chinese manufacturers with global markets, setting the standard for excellence in international trade and digital innovation.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional value to our partners through comprehensive trade solutions, innovative mobile applications, and unwavering commitment to quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction in every interaction.

What We Stand For

Core Values

These principles guide every decision we make and every relationship we build.

Integrity First

We conduct business with honesty, transparency, and ethical practices. Every transaction, every partnership, and every commitment is built on a foundation of trust.

Excellence Always

We pursue excellence in everything we do, from product quality to customer service. We continuously improve our processes and deliver results that exceed expectations.

Innovation Drives

We embrace change and continuously seek innovative solutions. Our mobile applications and business processes evolve with technology and market demands.

Partnership Success

We view our clients and partners as collaborators. Their success is our success, and we work together to achieve mutual growth and prosperity.

Global Perspective

We celebrate diversity and respect cultural differences. Our global outlook enables us to understand and serve markets across continents effectively.

Responsibility

We take responsibility for our actions and their impact on stakeholders, communities, and the environment. Sustainable practices guide our operations.

Our Journey

Company History

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted global partner.

2009

Founded in Liuyang, Hunan Province, with a vision to connect Chinese manufacturers with international markets through reliable trade services.

2012

Expanded our product portfolio to include electronic products and agricultural by-products. Established first partnerships with European distributors.

2015

Launched our e-commerce division, enabling 24/7 online sales capabilities and reaching customers across multiple global platforms.

2018

Opened our dedicated supply chain management and warehousing facility, enhancing our ability to serve international clients with efficiency.

2021

Entered the mobile application market with our first business management app, published on Google Play and Apple App Store.

2024

Reached milestone of 500+ international partners across 50 countries, with over 10 million mobile app users worldwide.

Present

Continuously expanding our services and product range while maintaining our commitment to quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction.
